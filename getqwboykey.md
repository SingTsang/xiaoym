# 注册企业微信及获取群机器人webhookkey


1. 注册

<img src="https://i.ibb.co/zS7zffw/12-40-38-093874b867d73dd0790c7ed940899121-aa03fb4f8.png" alt="image-20230904124038146" style="zoom: 25%;" />

2. 登录企业微信，并关注插件，以便在微信上可以收到推送消息

<img src="https://i.ibb.co/Jz0D1SZ/12-57-49-eb2468a0c695661b9ceb0b045cef4619-16e090a97.png" alt="image-20230904125749474" style="zoom:25%;" />
